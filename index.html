How Zuvuya Came to Be

"The voyage of the best ship is a zig zag line of a hundred tacks."

— RW Emerson

I was gifted the Dreamspell in 1992.

In 1978 I met Jeff Green who would later become quite famous after his first book Pluto: The Evolutionary Journey of the Soul was published in 1985.

Our meeting was quite happenstance and rather humorous. I had purchased Esoteric Astrology and The Encyclopedia of Medical Astrology at a Theosophical Bookstore in Seattle Washington.

Reading Esoteric Astrology (AABailey) didn't require any knowledge of the symbols, signs, or aspects relative to astrology.

The Encyclopedia of Medical Astrology was a different story.

It is an exhaustive study. Mind boggling in contemplating what must have gone into such an undertaking and I couldn't make head nor tails out of the symbols or signs.

I went hunting for an Astrology 101 class and one day walked into Astrology Et Al which had a storefront in the University District.

That was in 1978.

I was shown the list of classes they offered and Evolutionary Astrology caught my eye. I asked about signing up for Jeff's class and was told rather sternly that it would better if I took a beginners class first. I noted the time and day of Jeff's class and well, I just showed up. I asked Jeff if I could sit in on his class given my limited understanding of astrology and he said fine. So I sat with about 6 or 7 other students well before he caught the wave to fame.

While with Jeff I attended many astrology conferences and met some very fine and diverse astrologers I was never drawn to pursue astrology as a profession.

We separated in 1988, I relocated to Northern California and continued my self guided study of astrology until I was gifted the Dreamspell.

What I hadn't found through my extensive astrological studies I found through the Dreamspell. In much the same way I approached studying astrology, I began studying the Dreamspell. In April of 2024 I put the two together and created the Zuvuya House System.

In my mind this house system is a natural and much needed progression in astrological thought and simplifies the complexity of the Dreamspell.

I am very pleased to be able to introduce it.

Laurie Burnett
Lake County California 2025

The Mayan Long Count and the Dreamspell Count

The Mayan Long Count and the Dreamspell Count are two distinct counts rooted in the Mayan Tzolkin yet differ in structure and origin.

The Mayan Long Count

The Mayan Long Count is a part of the Classical Mayan Calendric system. Its epoch began on 0.0.0.0.0. corresponding to August 11th 3114 BC (Gregorian Calendar) and September 6th 3114 BCE (Julian Calendar).

It's been used historically for recording dates and texts that can be found in monuments dating back thousands of years.

The Dreamspell Count

Dreamspell : The Journey of Timeship Earth was designed by Jose and Lloydine Arguelles.

Introducing new concepts and spiritual terminology not present in the traditional Mayan system, it became a cornerstone of the New Age Movement giving rise to the Harmonic Convergence August 16th and 17th 1987. Considered by some to be a more idealized and non-linear perspective, designed for spiritual exploration and global harmony, its epoch began on July 26th 1987 aligning with the heliacal rising of Sirius.

The heliacal rising of Sirius is an annual event and occurs at its first reappearance in the sky after a period of being hidden by the Suns glare.
