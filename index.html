The Mayan Calendar

The Tzolk'in

The Tzolk'in combines 20 day signs with 13 tones, creating a 260-day sacred calendar that has guided seekers for millennia in understanding cosmic patterns and personal energy.

The Mayan Long Count

The Long Count measures time in cycles, revealing how everything returns to itself while simultaneously evolving — like the acorn already containing the oak. This ancient system reveals profound insights into cosmic cycles.
