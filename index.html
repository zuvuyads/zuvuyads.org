Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't Zuvuya require my birth time?

Unlike traditional astrology, the Zuvuya House System does not require birth time or place to generate your core chart. However, planetary placements are a crucial element and benefit from knowing your time and place of birth for enhanced accuracy.

What if I don't know my birth time?

If you don't have exact time and/or place of birth, a core Zuvuya chart can still be generated. In that case, planetary positions are calculated for solar noon and a default location, providing a reliable overview of your Zuvuya motion pattern.

How does Zuvuya differ from other astrological systems?

Zuvuya repositions planetary placements within a framework that integrates Mayan calendar wisdom. We read from the inside out because the soul doesn't receive motion — it remembers it.

Is Zuvuya accessible to beginners?

Yes. While the implications are profound, the system is designed to be straightforward and accessible to both beginners and experienced practitioners, with clear insights into your cosmic memory.
